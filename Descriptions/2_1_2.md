### 🛠️ Step 2: Assemble MGN rail on aluminium profile

In this step, we will mount the pre-assembled MGN rail onto the aluminium profile and secure it properly.

### 📝 Instructions
1. Place the MGN rail onto the aluminium profile, ensuring all M3 T-nuts are inserted into the channel of the aluminium profile.
2. Align the beginning of the rail with the edge of the aluminium profile as indicated.
3. Using a 2.5mm Allen key, tighten the screws at the ends of the MGN rail where the alignment parts are located.
4. Using a 2.5mm Allen key, tighten the remaining screws holding the MGN rail.
