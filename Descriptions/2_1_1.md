### 🛠️ Step 2.1: Y-Axis Rail Assembly
In this step, we will mount the MGN12 rail to the Y-axis extrusion. Ensure the extrusion is clean of any metal shavings before proceeding.

#### 📦 Bill of Materials (BOM)
| Qty | Part Image/Icon | Component Description | Specification |
| :--- | :---: | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | 🛤️ | **MGN12H Rail** | 400mm Linear Rail |
| 12 | 🔩 | **M3x8mm Screw** | DIN 912 (Cylindrical) |
| 12 | ⬡ | **M3 T-Nut** | Drop-in for 2020 |
| 2 | 🧩 | **Alignment Jig** | Printed Part (PLA/ABS) |

> **Pro Tip:** Lightly oil the T-nuts before sliding them into the extrusion to prevent them from binding.

---

### 📝 Instructions
1. Slide **12x M3 T-Nuts** into the top slot of the Y-axis extrusion.
2. Place the **MGN12 Rail** on top and loosely start all screws.
3. Use the **Alignment Jigs** at both ends to center the rail perfectly.
4. Tighten from the center outwards.
