### 🛠️ Step 1: Pre-assemble screws and T-Nuts on MGN rail

**<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #ff6b6b;">⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTE:</span>** <span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #ff6b6b;">This chapter builds the Y-axis frame beam. You will need to complete all steps in this chapter **twice** to create two identical frame beams for the printer.</span>

In this step, we will pre-assemble the M3x12mm screws and M3 T-Nuts onto the MGN12 rail before mounting it to the Y-axis extrusion. This ensures easier installation later.

#### 📦 Bill of Materials (BOM)
| Qty | Part Image/Icon | Component Description |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| 1 | ⚙️ | **MGN12 Rail 550mm** |
| 8 | 🔩 | **M3x12 SHCS Screw** |
| 8 | ⬡ | **M3 T-Nut** |
| 2 | 🧩 | **Alignment Jig (02_47__XP__Fix_MGN12_Rail___V#.#)** |

---

### 📝 Instructions
1. In the first and last hole of the rail, preassemble the alignment jig using M3x20 SHCS screws and M3 T-nuts
2. Preassemble M3x12 SHCS screws and M3 T-nuts with a gap off two holes in between, as shown.

---

### ℹ️ **INFO**
**<span style="font-size: 1.1em;">The Alignment Jig is used only for aligning the MGN rail onto the aluminium profile. After the rail is secured, the Alignment Jigs will be removed, and the M3x20 screws will be replaced with M3x12 screws.</span>**
