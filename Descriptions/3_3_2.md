### 🛠️ Step 2: Y-Axis Motors Mounting

In this step, we will mount the Y-axis motors with pulleys onto the fixing plates.

---

### 📝 Instructions
1. An anti-vibration gasket made of silicone is placed between stepper motors and mounting plate
2. Assemble motors onto the plate using FHCS M3x10 screws and a 2mm Allen key
3. **<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">Ensure motors are positioned correctly on the plate according to LEFT and RIGHT assembly positions on the 3D printer frame.</span>**
4. **<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">The motor cable connector should always be oriented to face the 8mm passthrough hole in the mounting plate</span>**
