### 🛠️ Step 1: Y-Axis Belt Tensioners Preassembly

In this step, we will preassemble Y-Axis belt tensioners.

#### 📦 Bill of Materials (BOM)

| **Qty** | **Part Image/Icon** | **Component Description** |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **2** | ⚙️ | **Y-Axis belt tensioner** |
| **2** | ⚙️ | **GT2 20 teeth 9mm idler** |
| **2** | 🔩 | **BHCS M5x25 Screw** |

---

###  Instructions
1. Install a 20-tooth GT2 idler for a 9mm wide belt on each Y-Axis tensioner
2. The GT2 idler is secured with an BHCS M5x25mm screw, which can be tightened using a 3mm Allen key
3. The screw head must always be oriented toward the inside of the printer, which is why two assembly versions are needed
