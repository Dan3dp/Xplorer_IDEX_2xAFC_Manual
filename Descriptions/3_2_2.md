### 🛠️ Step 2: Rail Installation on XY-Joints

In this step, we will install the linear rail onto the assembled XY-Joints.

---

### 📝 Instructions
1. Place 550mm long MGN12 rail on the XY-Joints
2. At each end, secure the MGN12 rail with two SHCS M3x16 screws and two flanged M3 nuts
3. A 2.5mm Allen key and a 5.5mm wrench will be needed for tightening
