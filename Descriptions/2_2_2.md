### 🛠️ Step 2: Assemble MGN Rail on aluminium profile

In this step, we will mount the pre-assembled MGN rail onto the aluminium profile and secure it properly.

### 📝 Instructions
1. Place the MGN rail onto the aluminium profile on the side with the 2.5mm deep milling, ensuring that all M3 T-nuts are inserted into the channel of the aluminium profile.
2. Place the beginning of the rail **32mm away from the edge** of the milling, as indicated.
3. Using a 2.5mm Allen key, tighten the screws at the ends of the MGN rail where the alignment parts are located.
4. Using a 2.5mm Allen key, tighten the rest of the screws holding the MGN rail.

### ℹ️ **INFO**
**<span style="font-size: 1.1em;">The alignment jigs ensure perfect positioning of the MGN rail on the aluminium profile. Make sure the rail is straight and properly seated before tightening all screws.</span>**
