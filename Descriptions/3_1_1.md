### 🛠️ Step 1: XY-Joint Assembly

In this step, we will assemble the XY-Joint for the IDEX gantry system.

#### 📦 Bill of Materials (BOM)

| **Qty** | **Part Image/Icon** | **Component Description** |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **2** | ⚙️ | **Carriage Y-Axis** |
| **2** | ⚙️ | **Fixation X-Axis Rail** |
| **2** | ⚙️ | **Fixation Y-Axis Belt** |
| **8** | 🔩 | **FHCS M4x16 Screw** |

---

**<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #ff6b6b;">⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTE:</span>** <span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #ff6b6b;">This XY-Joint assembly needs to be completed **twice** to create two identical joints for the IDEX gantry system.</span>


### 📝 Instructions
1. Take four FHCS M4x16 screws, apply a drop of Loctite 243 to the threads, and assemble the parts to form the joint for the gantry as shown using a 2.5mm Allen key.
2. Tighten the screw thoroughly.
