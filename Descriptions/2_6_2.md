### 🛠️ Step 2: Assemble side frames to back frame

In this step, we will attach the side frames to the back frame to create the main structure of the printer frame.

### 📝 Instructions
1. Lay the frame back on a flat surface as shown below.
2. Slide one of the two side frames into position as indicated.
3. **<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">Make sure the side frame is perfectly aligned with the upper aluminium profile of the back frame at its edge.</span>**
4. Using a 5mm Allen key, access the screws head through the passthrough holes in the pillars and tighten the 4x BHCS M8x16 screws.
5. Proceed the same way with the second side frame.

### ℹ️ **INFO**
**<span style="font-size: 1.1em;">Ensure perfect alignment before tightening screws to maintain frame squareness and structural integrity.</span>**
