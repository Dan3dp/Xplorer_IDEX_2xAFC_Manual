### 🛠️ Step 2: Assemble MGN rail on aluminium profile

In this step, we will mount the pre-assembled MGN rail onto the aluminium profile and secure it properly.


### 📝 Instructions
1. Place the MGN rail onto the aluminium profile, ensuring that the pass-through hole is positioned as indicated and oriented sideways
2. Place the beginning of the rail **25.5mm away from the edge** of aluminium profile, as indicated.
3. Using a 2.5mm Allen key, tighten the screws at the ends of the MGN rail where the alignment jigs are located.
4. Using a 2.5mm Allen key, tighten the rest of screws holding the MGN rail.
