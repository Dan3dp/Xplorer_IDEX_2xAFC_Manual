### 🛠️ Step 4: Assemble upper profiles

In this step, we will assemble the upper profiles to complete the backside frame structure. This step involves positioning and securing the aluminium profiles to create the final frame assembly.

### 📝 Instructions
1. Lay the aluminium profiles on a flat surface, oriented as shown below.
2. Assemble the MGN Z-Axis frame beam with the two 580mm long aluminium profiles as shown.
3. **<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">Pay attention to the orientation and position of the 580mm profiles relative to the MGN Z-Axis frame beam:</span>**

   **<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">• The 2.5mm milling should be positioned where the MGN rail is closer to the edge.</span>**

   **<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">• When the entire assembly is oriented as shown in the picture, the pass-through holes of both 580mm aluminium profiles must be on the left side.</span>**

4. Take 2x SHCS M8x35 screws, apply a drop of Loctite 243 to the threads, and tighten the aluminium profiles together securely to prevent rotation.

**<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #ff6b6b;">⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTE:</span>** <span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #ff6b6b;">**All M8 screws must be used with Loctite 243 to ensure rigid fixation. Alternatively, it is recommended to use threadlocker for all M8 screws for optimal assembly strength.</span>**
