### 🛠️ Step 1: Pre-assemble connection screws

In this step, we will pre-assemble the connection screws on the aluminium profiles to prepare for the final frame assembly. This ensures proper spacing and preparation for connecting the frame assemblies.

#### 📦 Bill of Materials (BOM)

| **Qty** | **Part Image/Icon** | **Component Description** |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **1** | ⚙️ | **Left Side Frame Assembly - assembled in Chapter 2.3** |
| **1** | ⚙️ | **Right Side Frame Assembly - assembled in Chapter 2.4** |
| **1** | ⚙️ | **Backside Frame Assembly - assembled in Chapter 2.5** |
| **4** | 🔩 | **SHCS M8x35 Screw** |
| **4** | 🔩 | **BHCS M8x16 Screw** |
| **4** | ⬡ | **M8 T-Nut** |

---

### 📝 Instructions
1. Take 4x ISO 7380 M8x16 screws, apply a drop of Loctite 243 to the thread, and preassemble them at the ends of the 580mm aluminium profiles
2. Leave between the screw head and aluminium profile face approx. **3mm space**
