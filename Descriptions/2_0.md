The Xplorer's frame is built entirely from 30×30 mm aluminum extrusion profiles, providing a strong, stable foundation for quality printing.

The assembly process is intentionally straightforward, with a simple and logical structure that’s easy to follow—even for first-time builders.

To ensure dimensional accuracy and perfect alignment,milled reference surfaces are used in all critical areas, so components align precisely during assembly.
The result is a frame that stays square, rigid, and vibration-resistant, even during high-speed or multi-toolhead operations.

This solid foundation is key to the Xplorer’s modular architecture and consistently reliable print quality.
