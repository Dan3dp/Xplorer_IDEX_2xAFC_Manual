### 🛠️ Step 1: Pre-assemble Y-Axis Beam

In this step, we will connect the Front Pillar with the Back Pillar by preassembling the Y-Axis beam between them. This creates the structural connection between the front and rear sections of the right side frame.


#### 📦 Bill of Materials (BOM)

| **Qty** | **Part Image/Icon** | **Component Description** |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **1** | ⚙️ | **Frame beam MGN Y-Axis - previously assembled in chapter 2.1** |
| **1** | ⚙️ | **Front Pillar - previously assembled in chapter 2.2** |
| **2** | ⚙️ | **Aluminium Profile 30x835mm** |
| **2** | ⚙️ | **Aluminium Profile 30x580mm** |
| **4** | 🔩 | **SHCS Screw 8x35mm** |
| **4** | 🔩 | **BHCS Screw M8x16mm** |

---

### 📝 Instructions
1. Lay the aluminium profiles on a flat surface, oriented as shown below.
2. Assemble the MGN Y-Axis frame beam with the front and back pillars by inserting its ends into the 2.5mm deep millings.
3. <span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">**Pay attention to the orientation of the MGN Y-Axis frame beam relative to the front pillars.** </span>
4. Take 2x BHCS M8x35 screws, apply a drop of Loctite 243 to the threads, and preassemble the MGN Y-Axis frame beam using a 6mm Allen key. <span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">**DO NOT TIGHTEN the M8 screws yet, as some play is necessaryt to complete the next assembly step.** </span>

---

**<span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #ff6b6b;">⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTE:</span>** <span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #ff6b6b;">**All M8 screws must be used with Loctite 243 to ensure rigid fixation. Alternatively, it is recommended to use threadlocker for all M8 screws for optimal assembly strength.</span>**
