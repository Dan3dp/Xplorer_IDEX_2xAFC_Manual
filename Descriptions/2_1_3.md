### 🛠️ Step 3: Remove fixation jigs

In this step, we will remove the alignment jigs that were used to position the MGN rail correctly on the aluminium profile. These jigs are no longer needed once the rail is securely fastened.

### 📝 Instructions
1. Using a 2.5mm Allen key, unscrew the screws holding the alignment parts.
2. Remove the alignment parts and the SHCS M3x20 screws from the assembly.
3. Use SHCS M3x12 screws to fix the rail where the alignment parts were previously.

### ℹ️ **INFO**
**<span style="font-size: 1.1em;">The alignment jigs are essential tools for ensuring perfect MGN rail alignment. Do not discard them - keep them in a safe place as they will be needed again for aligning the remaining MGN12 rails in the assembly process.</span>**
