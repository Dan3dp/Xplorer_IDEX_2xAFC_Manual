### 🛠️ Step 3: Final assembly of the right side frame



### 📝 Instructions
1. Slide the 580mm aluminium profiles into their designated positions: <span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">**the upper profile should be flush with the pillar ends, while the lower profile should be positioned 50mm from the pillar ends.**</span>
2. Now tighten all the screws holding the aluminium profiles together.
3. Use 6mm Allen key to tighten the 2x SHCS M8x35 screws holding the frame beam MGN for Y-Axis
4. Using a 5mm Allen key, access the screw head through the passthrough hole in the pillar and tighten the 4x BHCS M8x16 screws.
5. <span style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #c60808;">**Ensure that the 580mm aluminium profiles do not rotate while tightening the screws.**</span>
